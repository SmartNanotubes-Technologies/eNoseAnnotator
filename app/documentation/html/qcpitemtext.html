    Detailed Description

    A text label

    [Missing image html]

    Its position is defined by the member position and the setting of \ref setPositionAlignment. The latter controls which part of the text rect shall be aligned with position.

    The text alignment itself (i.e. left, center, right) can be controlled with \ref setTextAlignment.

    The text may be rotated around the position point with \ref setRotation.
